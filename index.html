about

Hi, my name is Julian. I’ve recently refreshed my skills through a myriad of accredited and non accredited courses, and am available to work on graphic and web design based projects. Having worked in a variety of settings and industries, I adapt quickly. Clear communication aligned with diligent organization enables me to manage expectations with a results-driven orientation.

From concept ideation to development, I use a nuanced approach including both experimentation and strategy to actualize a finished project. Rather than following trends, good design should be shaped by a product’s function, while fine details cultivate memorable experiences. My design practice aims to develop creative solutions without becoming overly complicated.
